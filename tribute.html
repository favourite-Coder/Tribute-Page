Michael Jackson

Forever dancing in the moonlight

Here's a timeline of Michael Jackson's life

  • 1969 - Michael Jackson's career takes off as the lead vocalist of the Jackson 5. The Jackson 5's debut single "I Want You Back" reaches No. 1 on the Billboard Hot 100


  • 1982 - Release of the iconic album "Thriller," which becomes the best-selling album of all time. The album's singles, including "Billie Jean" and "Beat It," dominate the charts and redefine pop music

  • 1983 - Michael Jackson's legendary performance of "Billie Jean" during the Motown 25 television special introduces the moonwalk to the world

  • 1984 - Jackson wins eight Grammy Awards in a single night, setting a record. The Pepsi commercial featuring Jackson leads to the infamous accident that results in his hair catching fire

  • 1987 - Release of the album "Bad," which generates multiple chart-topping hits such as "The Way You Make Me Feel" and "Smooth Criminal."

  • 1991 - Release of the album "Dangerous," featuring hits like "Black or White" and "Remember the Time." The music video for "Black or White" premieres simultaneously in 27 countries, garnering a record-breaking television audience

  • 1993 - Jackson is honored with the Grammy Legend Award for his contributions to the music industry. He is accused of child molestation, which leads to a highly publicized trial and legal battles

  • 1995 - Release of the double album "HIStory: Past, Present and Future, Book I," featuring the hits "Scream" and "You Are Not Alone."

  • 2001 - Jackson releases the album "Invincible," his final studio album of original material0

  • 2009 - Tragically, Michael Jackson passes away on June 25, 2009, leaving a profound impact on the music industry and millions of fans worldwide
